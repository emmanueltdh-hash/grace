Good to know

Your first questions, answered.

Every family’s journey is different. Here are a few helpful starting points.

How do I know which service my child needs?+

You don’t need to decide alone. We begin with a conversation and assessment, then recommend the most suitable support based on your child’s strengths and needs.

Can several therapists support my child?+

Yes. When helpful, our specialists collaborate around one coordinated care plan so goals and strategies remain consistent.

Are parents involved in the care plan?+

Always. We share practical strategies and regular progress updates so useful support continues between sessions.

Do you offer school and weekend support?+

Yes. School accompaniment and weekend support can be included where appropriate and available.
